Past Projects


YardSpace -- Ruby on Rails(postgreSQL)

Proof of concept of a social platform connecting travelers with hosts who have made their yards available to camp in. Features real-time messaging abilities, calendar booking system, friend requests, reviews, ratings and much more. The app is user tested, production ready, and slowly growing a user base


Studio Shifra -- Ruby on Rails(postgreSQL)

E commerce site made for a local artist client to display and sell their art work. Designed specifically with their product and market in mind. All features are handmade, which include a shopping cart for both users and guests, credit card payments, user information security measures, and a message board/reviews for users
